The State of our Environment

The United Nations has declared us in a state of emergency which means it is essential for us to start practicing healthier eco freindly habbits. We can all start by reducing the amount of plastic we consume, this is easily achieveable as there are many environmentally friendly products to be replaced with its plastic counter part. we can also make a big difference by upping our recycling game! we should all ensure that we are recycling into the correct bins and donating/recycling all of our old devices e.g (phones,computers, appliances and electronics). Another thing we can do to help our environment is to conserve as much clean water as possible, through the likes of short showers, fixing leaky pipes, and not leaving taps running for too long. this should help benifit the environment by reducing the demand for clean water which takes lots of energy and resources to purify
